Auto Tie Baler

Engineered for high-volume operations, the Auto Tie Baler automatically ties bales, dramatically reducing labor requirements. Two configurations allow you to choose single-material focus or multi-material versatility.

  • Automatic bale tying — minimal labor

  • Handles cardboard, paper, aluminum, plastics

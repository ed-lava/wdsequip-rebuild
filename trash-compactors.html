Our Compactor Lineup

The Right Compactor for Every Business

We offer six primary industrial trash compactor types, each designed for specific applications. All units reduce hauling costs, labor expenses, and storage space requirements while keeping your facility cleaner and more efficient.

Stationary Compactors

Ideal for high-volume waste generators that need maximum compaction and hauling reduction. Our stationary compactors reduce hauling by 3:1 and are environmentally friendly, lowering both labor costs and disposal fees.

  • Reduces hauling by 3:1
  • Odor control system included
  • Reduces labor costs & disposal fees
  • Minimizes required storage space

Self-Contained Compactors

Designed for wet waste applications where leakage could be an issue. The self-contained unit holds all liquids in a sealed container and allows continuous feeding capacity even while the compactor is operating.

  • Continuous feeding capacity during operation
  • Ground or dock-level loading options
  • Odor and pest control features
  • Sealed container — no leakage
Request a Quote Download Spec Sheet

Vertical Compactors

Compact and powerful, vertical compactors are perfect for smaller footprints. With a 30-second cycle time and motor options available, they offer a versatile and fast solution for medium-volume waste operations.

  • 30-second cycle time
  • 3HP or 5HP motor options
  • Convertible feed (front or rear)
  • Security chute for internal building access

Apartment Style Compactors

Engineered for multi-residential and high-rise applications where space is at a premium. With up to a 4:1 compaction ratio, this unit keeps shared trash rooms clean and orderly for residents and staff.

  • Up to 4:1 compaction ratio
  • Remote power unit option
  • Full-container indicator light
  • Chute-feed configuration

Auger Compactors

Non-hydraulic, all-electric operation makes Auger Compactors ideal for industrial environments. They compact materials up to five times faster than conventional hydraulic compactors, dramatically reducing handling time.

  • Non-hydraulic, all-electric operation
  • Compacts up to 5x faster
  • Ideal for industrial materials
  • Lower maintenance than hydraulic models

Pre-Crusher

When you need maximum density, the Pre-Crusher delivers. Providing up to 30% more compaction than your standard compactor with a fully automated continuous feed, this unit is built for the most demanding waste volumes.

  • Up to 30% more compaction than standard models
  • Fully automated continuous feed
  • Built for high-volume industrial operations
  • Reduces container pickup frequency

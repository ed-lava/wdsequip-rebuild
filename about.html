About IDEAL Compactors & Balers

IDEAL Compactors & Balers is a national equipment-leasing & purchasing supplier specializing in turn-key rental programs. We streamline the entire process — from installation and maintenance to repair and replacement — to enhance our customers' return on investment.

Operating since 1996, IDEAL has delivered waste and recycling solutions to clients nationwide. Our team emphasizes cost reduction through customized approaches tailored to individual business needs. As a women-owned business, we bring a client-first philosophy to every engagement.

  • Hands-on approach to every project
  • Customized solutions from design through installation
  • Leasing & purchasing options to fit any budget
  • Nationwide service network of qualified technicians
